About Us

Welcome to “El-Professor Food” where we dedicate ourselves to the exciting and vibrant world of food and beverages. We bring forth a legacy of excellence in the food and beverage industry that spans over five decades.

Since our establishment in 1970, we have built a reputation for delivering unparalleled quality and authenticity in every aspect of our culinary endeavors. Our unwavering commitment to preserving the essence of traditional recipes, combined with our passion for exploring various cultures, enables us to craft exceptional experiences.

By entrusting El-Professor Food with your F&B business, you embrace a partnership founded on expertise, innovation, and a shared love for remarkable gastronomic journeys. As we believe that everyone should have the opportunity to experience delicious, authentic food, regardless of their cooking skills or experience.

Over the years, our expansive team of creative, like-minded food and drink enthusiasts have created a meeting place for all those who find enjoyment in food and beverages. Where we offer a wide variety of services for our clients as well as a variety of services for F&B businesses.

Letter from the CEO

Dear Valued Customers,

Welcome to El-Professor Food, a one-stop hub for everything F&B related. We are a team of creative, like-minded food and drink enthusiasts dedicated to providing our customers with exceptional culinary experiences and innovative solutions.

Our passion for food is evident in everything we do, from food blogging and reviews to cooking workshops and franchising services. We are committed to preserving and promoting the culinary traditions of the world, while also embracing innovation and creativity. Here are just a few of our core values at El-Professor Food:


Authenticity: We are passionate about providing our customers with authentic F&B experiences. As well as our commitment to deliver the highest quality products and services.

Expertise: Our team of experts have the knowledge and skills to help you with all aspects of your F&B needs, from concept development to execution.

Innovation: We are always looking for new and innovative ways to improve our services and offerings. We are committed to providing our customers with unparalleled experience.

Customer focus: We are dedicated to customer satisfaction. We are always open to feedback and suggestions from our valued customers. We believe that our customers are our number one priority, and we are committed to providing them with the best possible service.

Whether you are a restaurant owner looking for help with concept development or a home cook looking to learn new cooking techniques, we’re here for you. offering a wide range of services to meet your individual needs, and we are always happy to go the extra mile to ensure your satisfaction.

We invite you to explore our website and learn more about our services. We are excited to partner with you to create memorable experiences.
